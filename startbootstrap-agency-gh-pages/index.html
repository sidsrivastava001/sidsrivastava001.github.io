About

PrepSAT is a group of motivated, experienced, and talented SAT tutors from High Technology High School with an average SAT score of 1580. All of our tutors have experience teaching a variety of subjects including, mathematics, english, computer science, and even the MCVSD entrance exam.

What really differentiates us, however, is that we were just in your shoes. Whether it be trying to gain an extra 30 or 300 points, as students who have recently taken the SAT, we've gone through it all. Through our SAT experience, we understand the various problems that students face, problems that adult tutors can't relate to or address. Because we are able to understand our students, we can directly address their struggles with strategies and solutions that have worked for us when we were preparing for the SAT.
